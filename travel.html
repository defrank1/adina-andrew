Getting There

From Union Station: Take the Red Line west until Gallery Place. Transfer to the Green Line toward Branch Avenue. Exit one stop later at Waterfront, and walk west along M Street and Maine Avenue until you reach the hotel at 7th Street.

From National Airport: Take the Yellow Line north until L'Enfant Plaza. Transfer to the Green Line toward Branch Avenue. Exit one stop later at Waterfront, and walk west along M Street and Maine Avenue until you reach the hotel at 7th Street.

From Dulles International Airport: Take the Silver Line east to L'Enfant Plaza, transfer to the Green Line toward Branch Avenue. Exit one stop later at Waterfront, and walk west along M Street and Maine Avenue until you reach the hotel at 7th Street.

By car: Please find garage arrangements through your hotel, or at The Wharf through this link.

Already in DC? Take the Metro to Waterfront, and walk west along M Street and Maine Avenue until you reach the hotel at 7th Street. Alternatively, take the D40 bus from anywhere along 14th Street directly to the hotel.
