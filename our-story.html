Our Story

Nobody can remember when we first met. We both agree it was sometime in Fall 2019, when our DC friend groups started showing up at each other's parties in Columbia Heights. We will ALWAYS have Sarah and D'Anieri to thank for that. Sometime in February or March, we all made plans to start a kickball team that Spring, which gave Andrew an opportunity to friend Adina on the only social media of hers he could find — Facebook. But then, the pandemic hit.

About a year and a half later, we matched on a dating app. Adina greeted Andrew with the nickname she knew Andrew by from her friends — "Transit Andy!" Our first date was far from picturesque. It was a cold, rainy night in the makeshift streatery outside of Hank's Oyster Bar in Dupont, in the middle of the Omicron wave.

After the second date, Andrew knew Adina was different. But it took Adina until the third date — cooking dinner at Andrew's — to feel the same way.

Over the next few months, we fell in love, while both applying to grad schools in and out of DC.
