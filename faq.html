Questions & Answers

What is Black Tie Preferred?

For gentlemen: If you own a tuxedo, this is your moment to wear it. For those without, a dark suit, white shirt, black shoes, and a dark tie can work.

For ladies: Full-length gowns as well as formal cocktail dresses are welcome.

Can I bring my children?

Unless we have told you otherwise, we won't be able to accommodate children at the wedding. Please let us know if you have any questions.

Will the wedding be outdoors?

No, but there will be balconies accessible at the cocktail hour and reception.

Are plus ones allowed?

Please check your invitation, as only guests whose names are on the invitation are invited.
